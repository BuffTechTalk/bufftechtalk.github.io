ABOUT US

Buff TechTalk is a new community for sharing knowledge about Information Technology at WT. It is an open platform for all campus members to explore and discover their passions within the technology field. We regularly:

- invite research scholars to share hot research topics
- invite industrial engineers to share working experience in the industry
- provide coding competitions focusing on daily-life problems for our students to practice programming skills and encourage them to transform a bright idea into a successful business

Should you have any questions/suggestions, or if you would like to be our guest speaker, please do not hesitate to contact us:
